Precision and Safety: Non-Conductive Labeling for Critical Systems

Safety in a data center is not just about fire prevention; it’s about protecting sensitive electronics. Unlike metal plates, our two-layer polymer engraved labels are complete dielectrics. This is a critical safety factor when marking power supply units, busbars, or any area with high electrical density.
